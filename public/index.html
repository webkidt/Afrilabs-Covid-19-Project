Best Practices

  • Work»
  • ⚬Ensure that any staff with symptoms of the COVID-19 self-isolate and alerts the authorities.
  • ⚬Try as much as possible to reduce physical meetings and have them virtually instead.
  • ⚬Encourage staff to work remotely.
  • ⚬Make available the tools to make virtual communication possible and educate your staff on its usage.
  • ⚬Set up an effective feedback system to collect ideas and concerns from your staff, review every suggestion and implement if necessary.
  • ⚬Adapt methods to keep the teamâ€™s morale high.
  • ⚬Send regular emails to staff, encouraging, showing support and acknowledging the stress and anxiety that the current situation is causing.
  • ⚬Cut down on expenses and activities to preserve core operations.
  • Travel»
  • ⚬Cancel trips, best measure to avoid the spread of COVID-19.
  • ⚬It is prudent for travellers who are sick to delay or avoid travel to affected areas, in particular for elderly travellers and people with chronic diseases or underlying health conditions.
  • ⚬As for any travel, travellers are also advised to follow proper food hygiene practices, including the five keys for food safety, as well as recommendations to reduce the risk of transmission of emerging pathogens from animals to humans in live markets.
  • ⚬Travellers should practise proper personal hygiene and ensure to keep distance between them and the next passenger, also avoid physical contact with anyone.
  • ⚬If any staff recently returned from any trip, they should self-isolate for 14 days.
  • Health»
  • ⚬Keep your home surroundings clean and disinfect regularly.
  • ⚬Avoid physical contact with anyone which includes kissing, hugging, handshakes.
  • ⚬Avoid crowded places, if the need arises to be physically present at a place use a proper medical mask and a glove to avoid touching contaminated surfaces.
  • ⚬Ensure you cough and sneeze into your elbow.
  • ⚬Wash your hands thoroughly while singing the happy birthday song twice and apply an alcohol-based hand sanitizer regularly.
  • ⚬Stay hydrated.
  • ⚬Consume properly cooked food and ensure healthy food hygiene.
  • ⚬Ensure to take enough vitamins to strengthen your immune system.
